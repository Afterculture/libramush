The meetme command is used to hook up with other players for RP.  You can easily join another player's location or bring them to you.

    %xcmeetme <list of names>%xn - Invites others to join you.
    %xcmeetme/bring%xn - After receiving an invitation, bring them to you.
    %xcmeetme/join%xn - After receiving an invitation, go to them.
