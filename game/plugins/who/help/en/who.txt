The %xhwho%xn command shows you which other players are connected to the game and information about them.

   %xcwho%xn - Shows the who list.
   %xcwhere%xn - Shows the who list including character locations.
   %xchide%xn and %xcunhide%xn - Controls whether your location shows up on the who list.
      There is no way in Ares to completely hide yourself from the who list or room
      descriptions (i.e. no dark/hidden power.)
