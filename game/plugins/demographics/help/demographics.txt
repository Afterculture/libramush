Demographics commands are used to set your basic character info.  Some items may not be changed after your character has been approved.

    %xcfullname <name>%xn
    %xcgender <male/female/other>%xn
    %xcheight <height>%xn
    %xcbuild <build - athletic, wiry, slim, pudgy, etc.>%xn
    %xchair <hair color>%xn
    %xceyes <eye color>%xn
    %xcbirthdate <yyyy-mm-dd>%xn or %xcage <years>%xn
