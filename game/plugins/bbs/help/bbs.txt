bbs
bbs <board>
bbs <board>/<post #>
bbs/post <board>=<subject>/<body>
bbs/reply <board>/<post #>=<reply>

bbs/new
bbs/catchup <board, or 'all'>

bbs/edit <board>/<post #> - Grabs the existing post text.
bbs/edit <board>/<post #>=<new text> - Replaces post text with the new text.
bbs/delete <board>/<post #>
bbs/move <board>/<post #>=<new board>

In all cases, <board> means the board name or number.
