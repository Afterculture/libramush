The Request system is used to submit requests to the admins. You can track the status of your request, as well as who it's assigned to.

    %xcrequest <title>=<description>%xn - Submits a request
    %xcrequest <#>%xn - Views details of a request.
    %xcrequest/comment <#>=<comment>%xn - Adds a comment to a +request.
    
    %xcrequests%xn - Views your active requests
    %xcrequests/all%xn - Views all of your requests, even closed ones.
