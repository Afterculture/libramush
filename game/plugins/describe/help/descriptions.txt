Every character, room and exit has a %xhDescription%xn that tells you what you see when you look at it.

Description commands:

   %xhLook%xn - Look around you.
   %xhDesc%xn - Set a description.
   %xhShortdesc%xn - A shorter description used for at-a-glance viewing.
   %xhOutfit%xn - Manage multiple outfits for your character's description.
   %xhWear%xn - Wear your outfits.
